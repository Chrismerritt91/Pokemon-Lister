Welcome to Pokepedia

Pokepedia is a place where trainers of any region can find their favorite pokemon and maybe learn something new about them. By selecting a region above you will be given a list of the pokemon who origins come from that region. If you already have a pokemon in mind feel free to use the search bar and enter their name or number.Once you have made your selection you will be taken to their information page. If you would like you can also star your favorite pokemon, and by clicking the favorites tab, you will be taken to a page of all of your favorites.
